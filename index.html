Bio

Born and raised in middle America, I started my career off as a merchant mariner. Once I started working shoreside I quickly embarked on a career in delivering digital solutions to provide value and meet the needs of people and companies.

I've experience in many aspects of a software offering, including strategy, management, sales, delivery and development. My interest now is on how the disciplines of design, development and especially product management work and come together to solve what Marty Cagan refers to as the four product risks:

  • Value Risk
  • Usability Risk
  • Feasibility Risk
  • Business Viability Risk
